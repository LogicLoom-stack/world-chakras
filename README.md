# Chakra: Global Resonance & Energy Monitor (v2.0)

**A Generative Study in Data-Driven Empathy and Global Sentiment**

`Chakra_Core.v2` is a generative "Energy Monitor" that translates the volatility of global news cycles into a visual, meditative experience. By treating the world’s news-grid as a vibrational load, this project explores how shifting geopolitical and social narratives affect our collective digital resonance.

### The Concept
Inspired by the intersection of Vedic philosophy and Cyberpunk aesthetics, this piece visualizes an **Impact Vector** based on real-time narrative sentiment. The sculpture consists of seven modular sectors (Chakras), each represented by unique geometric Yantras and petal counts.

*   **Global Resonance Sync:** The system scans a synthetic news-grid. When positive milestones are reached (e.g., medical breakthroughs or ceasefires), the system enters a "Positive Resonance" state, cycling through a spectrum of light.
*   **Vibrational Load:** High-impact negative events (e.g., data breaches or economic crashes) trigger a "Thermal Alert," shifting the entire core into a high-intensity red/magenta state with increased rotational entropy.
*   **Macro Entropy:** A real-time calculation of visual disorder that fluctuates based on the severity and confidence of the incoming data stream.

### Technical Implementation
*   **Engine:** JavaScript / [p5.js](https://p5js.org/) in Instance Mode.
*   **Rendering:** WEBGL-accelerated 3D environment featuring a custom Starfield engine and procedural geometry.
*   **Design:** Modular UI panels with CSS backdrop-filters for a "glassmorphism" aesthetic, coupled with HSB/RGB color-interpolation for smooth energetic transitions.

### The "Why"
As we move toward a future defined by Agentic AI and massive data streams, `Chakra` asks: *How can we design interfaces that don't just show us data, but help us feel its weight?* It is an experiment in building socio-technical systems that prioritize human resonance over raw information density.
